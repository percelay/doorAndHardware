About 

Established in 1909, Door and Hardware Supply, Inc. is centrally located in Middlesex County, New Jersey. Operating from a 50,000-square-foot facility within the Perth Amboy Urban Enterprise Zone, we offer our clients the benefit of a reduced 3.5% New Jersey State sales tax rate. We specialize in the precise coordination of Division 8 and 10 door, frame, and hardware packages. Our staff features licensed Architectural Hardware Consultants (AHC) and brings over 150 years of combined, industry-certified experience. We ensure all projects comply with NFPA 101 Life Safety and NFPA 80 fire safety codes, delivering unparalleled knowledge, service, and meticulous attention to detail.
